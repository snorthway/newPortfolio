Productivity

I tracked the amount of time I spent on every single out-of-class activity and graphed the results with d3.js. It was meant as an exercise in consistency, and later as an exercise to learn d3. It taught me a lot about my habits, and now you can check them out as well. Word of warning: it's not responsive yet, so it'll only look good if you're on something 960+ pixels wide.

Physarum Experiment

I conducted an experiment to attempt to simulate Boston's transportation systems with the emergent structures of slime mold physarum polycephalum. As part of the experiment I built an intervalometer and set up my camera to do a time-lapse of the process. I also wrote a minimum spanning tree algorithm which I implemented in d3.js.
